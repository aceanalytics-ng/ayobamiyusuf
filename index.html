About Me

I am a self-taught data analyst and business intelligence professional with a passion for uncovering gems hidden in data. My interest in this field began as a hobby around crunching numbers in Microsoft Excel and exploring the various charts that could be created with these numbers, but I quickly realized the potential impact that data can have on making informed decisions in various industries. I have gained experience in various industries, applying my skills in data analysis, business intelligence, and data visualization to help organizations make better decisions. My proficiency in data analysis softwares such as Excel, Power BI, Tableau, and SQL, has allowed me to extract insights from large data sets, leading to data-driven decision-making.

In my free time, I not only continue to research and learn about new techniques and technologies related to data analysis but also enjoy playing and writing music. I am also an avid gamer, spending time exploring different virtual worlds and interacting with other players. This balance of professional and personal interests helps me to approach problem-solving with a diverse and creative mindset.
